\part{Cognitive Architecture}
\label{part:cognitive}

\epigraph{The singularity is less an explosion of raw machine capability and more a
transition in representational infrastructure.}{}

\noindent Part~\ref{part:cognitive} develops the Human Cognitive Singularity argument
as the central theoretical object of the monograph. Intelligence is treated as a
scaffolded amplification process grounded in representational breakthrough rather than
metaphysical discontinuity. The Ellul constraint on technical opacity is formalized
in the vocabulary of admissibility and trajectory visibility.
